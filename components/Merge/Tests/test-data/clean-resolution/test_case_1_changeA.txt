
### 1. Merge Evaluation Test Suites
The paper "Evaluation of Version Control Merge Tools" by the University of Washington provides a detailed methodology for testing merge tools.
It describes scenarios where:
- Parent/Base Document: The original document before any changes.
- Change A: A modification made in one branch.
- Change B: A separate modification made in another branch.
- Merge Result: The expected outcome.
The paper includes various examples of merge scenarios.
